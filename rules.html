Official Project Rules

⚙️ 1. General Rules § 1
1.1By joining the server, you automatically confirm that you have read and agree to follow the rules of our server.
1.2Ignorance of the rules does not exempt you from responsibility.
1.3Rules may be updated or clarified without notifying members.
💬 2. Communication & Conduct § 2
2.1Be polite and respectful to all members.
2.2Prohibited: insults, bullying, humiliation, threats, provocation, and toxicity.
2.2.1Only excessive insults are prohibited; insults targeting family members are fully prohibited.
2.4Prohibited: discrimination based on nationality or religion.
2.5Conflicts are resolved through moderation, not publicly.
2.6Swearing is permitted, but not directed at individual members.
2.7Spam, flood, caps, meaningless messages, and off-topic — only in the #flood channel.
2.8Prohibited: criticism of project staff.
2.9Complaints about staff can be submitted in the #complaints channel.
2.10Prohibited: pinging members and staff without a valid reason.
🚫 3. Content & Materials § 3
3.1Prohibited: NSFW, pornography, shock content, gore, violence.
3.1.1Exception: unrealistic gore, violence, and blood from video games. NSFW and pornography are strictly prohibited regardless.
3.2Prohibited: distribution of malicious files and virus links.
3.3Prohibited: political and religious disputes.
3.4Prohibited: any content that violates Discord ToS.
3.5Memes should only be posted in the #memes channel.
3.6Screenshots and videos — only in the #screenshots-and-videos channel.
📢 4. Nicknames, Avatars & Statuses § 4
4.1Nicknames must be readable, without insults or provocations.
4.2Prohibited: names imitating project staff (e.g., 'Admin', 'Moderator', 'Bot').
4.3Prohibited: avatars with NSFW, violence, insults, or political symbols.
4.4Statuses are also subject to these rules.
🧩 5. Advertising & Invites § 5
5.1Any advertising — only with permission from the project head.
5.2Prohibited: unauthorized invite distribution, DM promotion, and spam links.
5.3Violation of these rules will result in permanent ban without warning.
🔊 6. Voice Channels § 6
6.1Use a proper microphone without noise, echo, background sounds, or max-volume music.
6.2Music and soundpad are allowed only with the consent of all participants.
6.3Prohibited: streaming NSFW, gore, pornography, violence, and shock content.
6.4Other rules also apply to voice channels.
⚖️ 7. Responsibility § 7
7.1Any violation may result in a warning, mute, kick, or ban — depending on severity.
7.2Repeated violations increase the punishment.
7.3Attempts to evade punishment (alt accounts, VPN, etc.) — ban of all linked accounts.
7.4Moderation decisions are always final. If you believe a punishment is unfair — submit an appeal ticket.
🛡️ 8. Moderation § 8
8.1Moderators are required to explain each action only in tickets.
8.2Complaints about moderators are accepted only with evidence (screenshots, videos, etc.).
8.3Moderation has the right to remove content without warning.
🗂️ 9. Miscellaneous § 9
9.1The degree of punishment is determined by the moderator.
9.2For repeated violations, a ×2 punishment from the previous one is applied.
9.3You have the right to submit an appeal ticket if you believe the punishment is unfair.
9.4In case of numerous violations, you will receive a permanent ban.
9.5If you hinder the project's development, raid, DDoS, etc. — permanent ban.
